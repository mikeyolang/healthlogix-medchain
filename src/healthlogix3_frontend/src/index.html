How It Works

Log in as a Nurse, Doctor, or Administrator to access role-specific features. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Click on the "Log In as Nurse" button if you are a nurse.
  2. Click on the "Log In as Doctor" button if you are a doctor.
  3. Click on the "Log In as Admin" button if you are an administrator.
